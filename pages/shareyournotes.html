How to Share Your Notes

Part 1: By the Sender

Step 1: Ensure your notes are clearly legible by taking high-quality photos. The clearer, the better.

Step 2: Compile the images into a PDF. You can use various online tools to create your PDF.

Step 3: Send the PDF to the email address provided below.

Step 4: In your email, include the following details:

  • Your Name
  • Semester
  • Institute/Branch
  • The Topic or Title of the Notes
Attach the PDF to your email.

Part 2: By the Receiver

Step 5: After a quality check, you will receive a response within 24 hours indicating whether your notes have been accepted.
